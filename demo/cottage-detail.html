🏡 About This Property

Escape to this stunning 4-bedroom lakefront cottage nestled on the pristine shores of Lake Rosseau, one of Muskoka's most sought-after lakes. Wake up to breathtaking sunrises over the water, spend your days swimming off the private dock, or take the canoe out for a peaceful morning paddle.

This beautifully appointed cottage features an open-concept main floor with soaring cathedral ceilings, a chef's kitchen, and a massive stone fireplace for cozy evenings. All four bedrooms have been designed with comfort in mind — the primary suite boasts lake views and a private ensuite. The wraparound deck is perfect for al-fresco dining or simply watching the sunset over the water.

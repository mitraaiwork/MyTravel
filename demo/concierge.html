Perfect! And what's the easiest way to get from Narita to Shinjuku? We land at 9am.
✨
Good timing — 9am is before the midday rush. You have three solid options:
  • Narita Express (N'EX) — ¥3,070/person, 90 min direct to Shinjuku. Most comfortable with luggage. Buy tickets at the airport JR counter. ✅ Recommended for you.
  • Limousine Bus — ¥3,200/person, 90–120 min depending on traffic. Door-to-door to major hotels. Good if your hotel is on the route.
  • Keisei Skyliner + JR transfer — ¥2,570/person, ~100 min. Slightly cheaper but one transfer at Ueno.

👉 For 2 people with luggage, the N'EX is the best value. Get the "N'EX Tokyo Round Trip" ticket for ¥5,000 total — that includes a return to the airport too!

Amazing, thank you! One more — are cherry blossoms actually blooming in mid-April?
✨
🌸 Here's the honest answer: mid-April is technically post-peak for classic sakura in Tokyo. Peak full bloom is usually March 28 – April 5.

That said — you're not out of luck at all:
  • Late-blooming varieties (yae-zakura, double-petal) are gorgeous in mid-April at Shinjuku Gyoen
  • Wisteria (fuji) starts blooming in late April — Kawachi Fuji Gardens is stunning
  • Ueno Park often has straggler blossoms and great hanami atmosphere even past peak

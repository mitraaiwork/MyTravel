✨ AI Trip Summary

Your Tokyo adventure was a masterclass in balancing the ancient and the ultramodern. From the misty forested path of Shinjuku Gyoen bursting with late cherry blossoms, to the pulsing neon energy of Shibuya crossing at rush hour — the geographic sequencing meant you experienced the full emotional range of the city without ever doubling back. The Nikko day trip on Day 4 stood out as the group's top-rated moment: the towering cryptomeria forests and Toshogu's gold-leaf gates left a lasting impression. Food was a highlight throughout — the spontaneous stop at the Uobei conveyor-belt sushi on Day 3 became a group favourite that no one had planned.

🌟 Top Moments
🌸
Shinjuku Gyoen at dawn — late sakura in full bloom
Arrived at 7:30am before the gates opened. The yae-zakura (double-petal) variety were at absolute peak. Zero crowds for the first hour.
🎨
TeamLab Planets — the waterfall room
Three floors of digital nature. The ankle-deep water room with falling light was the single most-photographed moment of the trip.
⛩
Nikko: Toshogu Shrine in morning mist
The 2-hour N'EX ride was 100% worth it. Mist through the cedar trees around the shrine complex was otherworldly.
🍶
Golden Gai — Albatross Bar, second floor
David's recommendation paid off. The eight-seat bar with a city-view window and ¥800 cocktails was a perfect first-night ending.

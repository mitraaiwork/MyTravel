MyTravel-AI Tip for Tokyo
Tokyo has an incredible culture of buying what you forgot — convenience stores (7-Eleven, FamilyMart) stock everything from umbrellas to charging cables. Don't overpack. Leave room for souvenirs — Narita Airport has a 23kg checked baggage limit on most carriers, and you'll likely shop in Harajuku and Akihabara.
